EXCITING NEWS FROM OUR SECTION SPONSOR!

On August 27, 2018 Ankura Consulting Group, LLC completed the acquisition of Navigant’s Disputes, Forensics and Legal Technology (DFLT) segment and Transaction Advisory Services (TAS) practice.

Together as one company, Ankura is a leading business advisory and expert services firm with over 1,400 employees in more than 30 offices worldwide. The capabilities, talent, and legal industry expertise of Navigant’s former practices strengthen and expand Ankura’s service offerings, which now include analytics & digital transformation; investigations & accounting advisory; litigation, arbitration & disputes; risk management & compliance advisory; strategy & operations; transaction advisory services; and turnaround & restructuring. 

This is exciting news for our sponsor, who will continue to provide the support and engagement you have come to expect from our valued network of Navigant DFLT experts, now under the Ankura name. Many of the section’s long-term committee members and some new Ankura faces will join us in Louisville and they look forward to connecting! If you have any questions, please contact Madalyn Messer-Brooks or Danny Van Horn.
